About Us

A Culinary Journey Through Tamil Nadu

Refresh Roadside Restaurant is a family-owned and operated restaurant that has been serving the Theni community for over 20 years. We are passionate about creating delicious, authentic Tamil Nadu cuisine that is made with fresh, local ingredients. Our menu features a variety of dishes, including dosas, idlis, vadas, sambar, rasam, and more.
